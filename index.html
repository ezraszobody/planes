Airplane Adventure Club
Airplanes are very cool and interesting. Studying them and playing them is always very fun. This club is to encourage people who like planes to commit to a whole plane club. When a kid draws a card, the card tells them something to do related to airplanes. I hope whoever joins will very much enjoy the club!

About What we do Meetings
This club is designed for around 4 years to 12 years. It is made so that we can do things related to airplanes. Wether it is building lego airplanes, playing passengers at an airport, etc. The Airplane Adventure club is to have lots and lots of fun!
Usually, we tend to draw a card from a deck. During that day, you must do what that card says to do. If you do not, you are suspended from the club for 2 days. Although this is mainly what we do, their will be other things. Talk to the president if you have any ideas.
On most occations we meet once a day but it may be longer. Ask the president when the next meeting will be. If you cannot make it to a meeting, let the president know. The meetings will be always inside the headquarters unless stated on the website.
