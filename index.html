The Hobbit is a fascinating adventure movie which is based on a novel. A hobbit named Bilbo Baggins gets caught in a quest. Dwarves had lost their home to a great dragon named Smaug. Dwarves love gold but so do dragons. When Smaug learned the dwarves had heaps of gold, he attacked them causing so much destruction. To take back their home, the dwarves needed a burglar. Bilbo fit the spot (Even though he didn't want to). They encounter many dangers including a necromancer, giant spiders, and even Smaug himself. Do watch the movies for this website will not spoil it for you!
